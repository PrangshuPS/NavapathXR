About NAVPATHXR

NAVPATHXR's AI Explorer is a cutting-edge platform that democratizes access to ocean data through AI-powered tools and interactive visualizations. We make complex oceanographic information accessible to researchers, educators, and ocean enthusiasts worldwide.

What is NAVPATHXR?

NAVPATHXR is an AI-powered platform that lets you explore oceanographic data from ARGO floats using natural language queries and interactive dashboards.

Where can I access the data?

The platform provides visualizations and insights from ARGO float datasets globally, with an initial focus on the Indian oceans. Users can explore profiles, trajectories, and parameter comparisons online.

How does it work

NAVPATHXR ingests NetCDF data, stores structured data in relational and vector databases, and uses AI models with RAG pipelines to translate user queries into interactive visualizations and reports.
